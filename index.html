How Super compares across the agent landscape

ChatGPT

ChatGPT excels at conversation, writing, and one‑off help. It’s increasingly agent‑like, but it’s still strongest as a general assistant rather than a durable operator for repeated local workflows.

Gemini

Google’s Gemini is moving fast into computer control, as widely reported in June 2026. It validates the direction of the market, but it’s not purpose‑built for small businesses reusing the same workflows daily.

Siri

Siri is a voice‑first assistant embedded in Apple devices. It’s convenient for quick actions, but limited for complex, multi‑step booking and quoting workflows.

Grok

Grok emphasizes opinionated answers and social context. It’s not designed for quietly operating CRMs, inboxes, and booking systems all day.

Folk & Orchids

Folk and Orchids represent niche or experimental tools in the broader agent market. They’re useful context, but not focused on hands‑on computer operation for local service work.
